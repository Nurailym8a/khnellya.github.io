Education

Dimash commuted to 32th school of Aktobe from the age of 6 and graduated musical class in 2009. In 2010, Dimash entered the Aktobe Music College named after. A. Zhubanov for training in the specialty “Opera singing”.From 2010 to 2013, Dimash won four major competitions: in Kazakhstan “Sounding Voices of Baikonur”, 2010 and “Zhas Kanat”, 2012; in Ukraine “Eastern Bazaar”, 2012; in Kyrgyzstan “Maikin Asia”, 2013.

In 2014, Dimash graduated from college and began his studies at the Kazakh National University of Arts with a degree in Stage Art. From April to December 2016, Dimash conducted his first concert tour of 25 concerts in 25 regions of Kazakhstan in honor of the 25th anniversary of Kazakhstan's independence, and also gave two concerts in Ufa and Kazan, Russia. He named his tour “Ұмытылмас күн” / “Unforgettable day” in honor of his own song of the same name.

On June 18, 2020, he graduated from the same university with a master's degree in Composition by defending his master's thesis with a perfect score and received a recommendation for admission to doctoral studies in music. In December 2022, Dimash became the owner of the Bolashak International Scholarship.
