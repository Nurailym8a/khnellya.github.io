Our team

This project was undertaken by two girls Nellya Khamudinova & Nurailym Adilkhan,from 2 different cities in Kazakhstan, who met while studying at the University of Bologna. We are united by a shared passion for language studies.

Through our coursework in "Language, Society and Communication," we seek to deepen our understanding of foreign languages and examine how communication shapes and influences society. By engaging with the "Language, Society and Communication" curriculum, we aim to explore the ways in which diverse languages impact society, and how communication is integral to cultural and social development.

We have come together to study this subject matter because we both recognize that cultural diversity presents unique challenges for communication, but we also believe that fostering mutual understanding can contribute to a more harmonious world. Furthermore, we are excited to apply the knowledge and insights gained throughout our academic journey.
